Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the internal software application ("Application") operated by Cultiva LLC ("Company") handles data. The Application is for the exclusive internal use of Cultiva LLC.

1. Information Accessed

The Application connects to Cultiva LLC's QuickBooks Online company through the Intuit API to read chart of accounts data and to post journal entries. It accesses only the accounting data necessary to perform internal production cost posting and reconciliation.

2. How Information Is Used

Accessed data is used solely to automate internal accounting processes between Cultiva LLC's ERP system and QuickBooks Online. No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party.

3. Data Storage

Authentication tokens and operational data are stored within Cultiva LLC's own Microsoft 365 and Azure environment, secured under Cultiva LLC's internal access controls.

4. Data Sharing

The Application does not transmit Cultiva LLC data to any external recipient other than Intuit QuickBooks Online, which is the system of record the Application integrates with.

5. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to Cultiva LLC accounting.
